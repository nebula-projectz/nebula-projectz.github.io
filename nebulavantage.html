Features
Everything you need
Built from scratch with GTK4 and Python. No Electron, no bloat.

Audio & Video

Full media playback via GStreamer. Play music, movies, and everything in between.

Smart Playlists

Create, edit, and organize playlists. Collapsible sidebar tree keeps everything tidy.

Dark & Light Mode

Fully themed dark and light interface with smooth transitions and accent colors.

Custom Icons

Assign custom PNG or JPG images as icons for playlists and tracks. Your library, your style.

Metadata Reading

Reads tags via Mutagen — title, artist, album, and duration shown automatically.

Native Performance

GTK4 + GStreamer. Hardware-accelerated, snappy, and lightweight by design.
